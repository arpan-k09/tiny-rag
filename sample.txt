Acme Store — Customer Policy Document

RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Customers may return any unused item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be returned in their original packaging with all tags attached. Proof of purchase is required for all returns. Digital products and downloadable content are non-refundable once accessed. Perishable goods cannot be returned. To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@acme.com with your order number. Approved refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days back to the original payment method.

SHIPPING

Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days and costs $4.99. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. Expedited shipping takes 1 to 2 business days and costs $12.99. We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs fees which are the responsibility of the customer. Orders are processed within 1 business day of placement. A tracking number is emailed once your order ships.

LOYALTY PROGRAM

Customers earn 1 loyalty point for every dollar spent. Points can be redeemed at a rate of 100 points equals $1 off any future order. Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity. Gold status is achieved at 500 lifetime points and includes free expedited shipping on all orders. Platinum status is achieved at 2000 lifetime points and includes a dedicated customer support line and early access to new products. Points are awarded after the return window closes on each order.

PRIVACY POLICY

Acme Store collects only the data necessary to process orders and improve the shopping experience. We never sell customer data to third parties. Data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only. Customers may request deletion of their personal data at any time by emailing privacy@acme.com. We are fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Cookies are used solely for session management and analytics.

CONTACT AND SUPPORT

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. You can reach us by email at support@acme.com, by phone at 1-800-ACME-HELP, or via live chat on our website during business hours. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent order issues, phone support is recommended for the fastest resolution.
